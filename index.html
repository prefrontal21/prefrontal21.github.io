步晓霖 | Xiaolin Bu

Hello! I am Xiaolin Bu. I am currently pursuing my master's degree in computer science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). My supervisor is Biaoshuai Tao. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree from SJTU in June, 2023.

My research focuses mainly on computational economics, including fair division problem and algorithmic game theory. I am also interested in formal verification.
